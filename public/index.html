About the campaign

We transform loneliness into social connection. With your support, seniors can experience trishaw rides, communal dining, art reminiscence workshops, bicycle repair training, and docent programmes, bringing joy and belonging.

Senior loneliness is a silent epidemic — and it is curable with kindness.
