Welcome to Cycling Without Age Singapore

Our mission is to bring joy to seniors and engage them in happy hours of community activities to reduce loneliness.

Today, 270,000 seniors aged 82 and above in Singapore experience loneliness. Loneliness not only increases the risk of developing depression & anxiety, but also increases the risk of death by 7%, while managing other health issues.

We transform loneliness into social connection. With your support, seniors can experience trishaw rides, communal dining, art reminiscence workshops, bicycle repair training, and trishaw docent programmes, bringing joy, purpose, and a sense of belonging!

Charity Transparency Award 2024

Cycling Without Age Singapore (CWAS) is honoured to be accorded The Charity Transparency and Governance (CTA) Award for 2024, organized by the Ministry of Culture, Community & Youth (MCCY). This award is a recognition of our commitment to promoting good governance and transparency in the charity sector, and it is aimed at highlighting the outstanding work of charities, motivating others to follow their lead.

CWAS wishes to share this achievement with our supporters, volunteers, and community partners. This award serves as proof of our continued efforts to maintain and enhance our standards of corporate governance and transparency. As a trusted charity, we remain dedicated to serving and empowering seniors by reintegrating them into the community through volunteerism.
